About The VioDetect

F.A.Q

  • What system is VioDetect?

    VioDetect is a system that automatically detects and warns against violent behavior through security cameras.

  • Why need the VioDetect system?

    In recent years, surveillance cameras are widely deployed in public places, and the general crime rate has been reduced significantly due to these ubiquitous devices. Usually, these cameras provide cues and evidence after crimes are conducted, while they are rarely used to prevent or stop criminal activities in time. It is both time and labor consuming to manually monitor a large amount of video data from surveillance cameras. Therefore, automatically recognizing violent behaviors from video signals becomes essential.

  • What features does VioDetect have?
    • Video streaming
    • Automatically detect violent behavior
    • Alerts when violent behavior is detected
  • Who can use the VioDetect system?

    Currently, the system is only in the research and development stage, so it cannot be put into operation yet. However, in the future, the system will aim for wide-scale deployment and anyone can use it.

  • The system was developed by who?

    The system was researched and developed by Doan Thi Mai, a student at the University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University, Hanoi. In the process of researching the system, there was also the help of Mr. Tran Quoc Long, along with all the teachers and friends at AILab.

  • When was the system developed?

    The system has been researched and developed since November 2020. And this is the first version of the system.
